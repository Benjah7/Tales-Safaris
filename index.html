    TOP PLACES TO GO

    Lake Turkana .

    Lake Turkana, located in the remote northwestern region of Kenya, is the world's largest desert lake and the fourth largest salt lake. Also known as the Jade Sea due to its striking turquoise color, it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site celebrated for its unique ecosystem and cultural heritage

    • Cultural Significance: Surrounding communities, such as the Turkana, Dassanech, and El Molo, have rich cultural traditions closely tied to the lake..
    • Unique Biodiversity: The lake is home to a variety of fish species, crocodiles, and birdlife, making it a vital ecological hotspot..
    • Archaeological Sites: The area around Lake Turkana is significant for paleoanthropological discoveries, providing insights into early human history..

    Mt.Suswa

    Mount Suswa is a remarkable volcanic mountain located in Kenya's Great Rift Valley, known for its unique double crater and rich biodiversity. This geological marvel offers an intriguing mix of adventure and natural beauty, attracting hikers, spelunkers, and wildlife enthusiasts.

    Double Crater: Explore the inner and outer craters, a distinctive feature that provides stunning vistas and interesting geological formations. Lava Caves: Discover the extensive network of lava caves, home to bats and an array of fascinating rock formations. Hiking Trails: Enjoy a variety of trails that lead through lush vegetation and provide panoramic views of the surrounding Rift Valley. Wildlife Viewing: Spot diverse wildlife, including baboons, zebras, and a variety of bird species, within the mountain's ecosystem. Cultural Encounters: Engage with the local Maasai communities and learn about their traditional way of life.

    Sagana Rapid Camps

    Sagana Rapid Camp is an adventure lover's paradise located in the heart of Kenya, near the town of Sagana. Nestled along the banks of the Tana River, this camp is renowned for its thrilling outdoor activities and stunning natural surroundings. Whether you're seeking an adrenaline rush or a tranquil escape, Sagana Rapid Camp offers a variety of experiences to suit every traveler..

    • White Water Rafting: Experience the thrill of navigating the Tana River's rapids, with expert guides ensuring both safety and excitement..
    • Kayaking and Canoeing: Enjoy the serene beauty of the river while paddling through calm sections and tackling challenging waters..
    • Rock Climbing and Abseiling: Test your skills on natural rock formations with guided climbs and descents..

    Kijabe Hills

    It's located in central Kenya near the town of Kijabe, offer a serene escape with stunning views of the Great Rift Valley. Known for their lush vegetation and diverse wildlife, the hills are a favorite destination for hikers, nature lovers, and adventure seekers.

    Scenic Hiking Trails: Enjoy a variety of trails that cater to different skill levels, offering panoramic views and the chance to explore Kenya's rich biodiversity. Bird Watching: The area is a haven for bird enthusiasts, with numerous species inhabiting the region. Cultural Significance: Kijabe Hills hold cultural importance for local communities and provide insights into traditional lifestyles. Proximity to Kijabe Forest: Explore the nearby Kijabe Forest, which adds to the area's natural charm and offers more opportunities for outdoor activities.
